Privacy Policy

How JVST collects, uses, and protects your information. Last updated January 12, 2026.

1. Introduction

JVST ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our websites, use our applications (including Indy AI, Tunes music service, Blade Card platform), or interact with our services (collectively, the "Services").

By using the Services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Services.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

We collect information you give us directly, such as:

  • Account details (name, email, password, student verification info for Blade Card)
  • Payment information (processed securely via third-party providers; we do not store full card details)
  • User-generated content (e.g., songs/creations in Tunes, queries/responses in Indy AI)
  • Feedback, support messages, or survey responses

Automatically Collected Information

We collect certain data automatically, including:

  • Device and browser info (IP address, device type, OS, browser version)
  • Usage data (pages visited, features used, time spent, interaction patterns)
  • Cookies and similar technologies (for analytics, preferences, and session management)

AI-Specific Data (Indy AI)

When using Indy, we collect conversation inputs/outputs to provide and improve the AI service. We do not use your personal conversations to train third-party foundation models without explicit consent. Anonymized usage patterns may help improve our internal models.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Services (e.g., personalize Indy responses, recommend Tunes content)
  • Process payments and manage Blade Card subscriptions/discounts
  • Communicate with you (updates, support, marketing with opt-out)
  • Detect/prevent fraud, abuse, or security issues
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Conduct research and analytics (aggregated/anonymized where possible)

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers (hosting, analytics, payment processors, email services)
  • AI infrastructure partners (for Indy processing; under strict DPAs)
  • Legal authorities if required by law
  • In connection with business transfers (merger, acquisition)

5. Data Retention

We keep your information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined here, or as required by law. Account deletion requests remove personal data (subject to backups/legal holds).

6. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your data
  • Opt out of marketing emails
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

To exercise rights, contact us at privacy@jvst.app. We respond within legal timeframes (e.g., 30–45 days).

7. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data (encryption in transit/rest, access controls). No system is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Services are designed and intended for middle school students and their families, which means they may be used by children under the age of 13. We comply fully with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), which protects the privacy of children under 13.

We do not collect personal information from children under 13 without first obtaining verifiable parental consent, except in very limited cases allowed by COPPA (such as for internal support operations that do not involve sharing or public disclosure of the data).

If we discover that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without proper parental consent, we will promptly delete that information. We never sell or rent children's personal information to third parties, and we only use it for purposes directly related to providing and improving our Services (such as personalizing Indy responses or managing Blade Card access), in strict compliance with COPPA.

Parents or guardians: If your child is using our Services, you can contact us at privacy@jvst.app to review, correct, or delete any personal information we have collected from your child, or to refuse further collection or use. We will respond to such requests in accordance with COPPA requirements.

For more information about COPPA and protecting children's online privacy, visit the Federal Trade Commission's website at ftc.gov/childrens-privacy.

9. International Transfers

Data may be transferred to and processed in the US or other countries. We use appropriate safeguards (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses) for transfers outside your region.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Material changes will be posted here with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
