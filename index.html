Built for queer people, not just built for everyone.

There's a difference between "everyone welcome" and actually designing with us in mind. JVST does the latter. Indy 3 uses affirming language by default, respects chosen pronouns, and doesn't center heteronormativity.

✓ Pronoun-aware responses, always

Too many AI tools misgender, make assumptions, or just feel cold and clinical. Indy is designed to feel warm, to not require you to justify your identity, and to be a space where you can just... exist.
